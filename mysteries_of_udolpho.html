About The Mysteries of Udolpho

The Mysteries of Udolpho by Ann Radcliffe, published in 1794, is a quintessential Gothic novel, featuring a young heroine, Emily St. Aubert, who faces numerous challenges and mysteries in a foreboding medieval castle.
