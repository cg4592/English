About Pamela

Pamela; or, Virtue Rewarded is a novel written by Samuel Richardson, first published in 1740. It is considered one of the earliest examples of the novel form, specifically the epistolary novel. The story revolves around a young maid named Pamela Andrews and her letters to her parents, which detail her attempts to ward off the advances of her lascivious master, Mr. B.
